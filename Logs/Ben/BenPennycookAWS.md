# Entry 1 - Starting out.

## What is AWS anyway?

AWS (Amazon Web Services) is the world's biggest cloud computing platform. With datacentres globally, it is used by huge companies such as:

- Sony, who spend $11M monthly
- Adobe, who spend $7.5M monthly
- Facebook, who spend $5.6M monthly

By being the first in the cloud computing market Amazon were able to set industry standards and never struggled to pick up big customers like the aforementioned Sony, who signed up in 2012 in order to handle a growing playerbase on their PlayStation consoles. This, combined with AWS having little to no competition for years after its birth, led Amazon to easily dominate the cloud infrastructure world.
